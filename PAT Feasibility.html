How to read this honest framing

Coupled + flat → the two device streams are the same heart on a shared phone clock; PAT variation tracks vascular tone → a provisional trend panel is buildable (experimental tier, never a BP number).

Coupled but drifting → clocks aren't perfectly locked; the "trend" is partly clock drift → needs per-segment correction first.

Not coupled → the files aren't on one clock (different phone / not simultaneous) → PAT is meaningless from these files. Re-capture both sensors in ONE Polar Sensor Logger session.

The absolute median lag = PEP + PTT + a constant clock offset, none separable without a BP cuff — so this spike measures coupling & stability, not a clinical value.
