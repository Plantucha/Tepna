CPAPDex `pressureChangePoints` — step-immune penalty scale (DEEP-AUDIT-II §6.1, the PEN_K/append half; the before/after self-contradiction was fixed earlier in PR #317). The BIC-like penalty `PEN_K·scale·log(span)` used the GLOBAL MAD for `scale`, but the global MAD is inflated by the very setting-steps the detector exists to find: (a) a clean 12→6→9 with 12 nights/regime cleared no penalty → ZERO change points, and (b) appending a later regime shifted the global median/MAD enough to ERASE an earlier change point (detection flipped on more data — the §6.1 append-non-invariance). The root cause was the SCALE ESTIMATOR, not `PEN_K`'s value. Replaced the global MAD with the MAD of SUCCESSIVE DIFFERENCES (`med(|Δ|)/√2`): level-shift-immune (the few large diffs AT the steps are outliers its median ignores, so it measures within-regime noise only), and `/√2` makes it EQUAL the old global MAD on pure white noise — so the zero-false-positive behaviour on a noise-dominated series (pressureEnvIqr, which the finding pins EMPTY) is preserved BY CONSTRUCTION while `PEN_K` stays 4.0. Verified on the real module over synthetics reproducing both corpus profiles: clean 12→6→9 → 2 CPs (was 0), append-invariance (`[10] → [10,20]`, the earlier CP preserved), and 0/300+ false positives on noise-only series at the pressureEnvIqr scale. New both-direction gate added. **Owner re-verifies** the exact corpus behaviour (epap95 10.7→6.8 caught, pressureEnvIqr empty) on the full 180-night SD card at release — the uploads/ subset is only 3 nights (below the 14-night detector minimum). Export-inert on the committed fixtures (their cross-night blocks were empty and stay empty; only CPAPDex bundle manifestHash + its code-gated fixtures re-stamped, verifiedUnder re-verified vs corpus).
