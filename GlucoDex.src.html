🍎Cronometer export
A daily summary (Date + nutrient totals) is date-matched to GlucoDex's per-day glucose to correlate net carbs / sugar / fiber against daily mean, TIR & CV — only where the calendar dates overlap. A timestamped servings export instead seeds meal markers automatically (carbs → light/medium/heavy).

🍽 Meal markers optional · v1.1 · time-of-day + carb category, applied every day
v1 ingests only the CGM file, so excursions are slope-detected and labelled unannotated. Add recurring meal times here and GlucoDex tags any excursion that starts in the −20→+75 min window after a meal — turning "an 8.1 mg/dL/min spike at 17:32" into "a heavy-carb dinner response".
