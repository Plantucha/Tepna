Synthetic Patient — Tepna coherence engine

Renders one fixed seeded patient (SubjectA · 45M · BMI 31) for N consecutive nights through the shared dex-patient-gen.js / synth-gen.js engine — the same generator PulseDex’s own Generate synthetic button uses. Deterministic (night i = seed 424242 + i·1000), Clock-Contract clean, with a shared severity random-walk so worse nights carry both more apnea and lower HRV. Export is the real Polar ISO;RR stream PulseDex ingests.

Life Stage & Health Profile

A parametric explorer: RR = mean + HF/LF/VLF sinusoids + AR(1) noise, seeded. Not the canonical coherence engine — single window, no sleep architecture, apnea, timestamps or cross-node coherence. Useful for quick spectral what-ifs; for fixtures and validation prefer the Coherence-engine mode. Export is a bare RR list (one interval per line) that PulseDex ingests.
