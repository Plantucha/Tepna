Synthetic ground truth — read before citing. One ~9-min apnea-cluster window per patient per arm. ECG: CohortFull.renderECGInt16 → real ECGDSP.analyze (Pan–Tompkins); truth = deviceRR. PPG: SYNTH.renderPPG → real PPGDSP.analyze (optical pulse); truth = SYNTH.buildRR in-window. Apnea beats are the generator's perfusion model (PPG pulse amplitude ×0.30 inside apnea/hypopnea windows). Recall is the PAT-corrected match rate of true beats; precision is the detected-beat match rate (PPG <1 = the known mild over-detection); SQI is the detector's own per-beat signal-quality. This certifies the ECG arm is yield-robust and isolates the PPG perfusion-yield gap and its rMSSD consequence; it is not a real-patient miss rate, which needs labelled simultaneous ECG+PPG under scored apnea. * ECG rMSSD bias is reconstructed from the synthetic ECG waveform, whose renderer fixes the R-peak at a constant intra-beat phase and so attenuates beat-to-beat rMSSD independently of yield — ECG is certified here for yield, not rMSSD fidelity (PPG truth + pulse-foot detection are both genuine, so the PPG bias is real).
