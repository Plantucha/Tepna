Synthetic ground truth — read before citing. Glycemia and HRV are co-generated from a shared nocturnal physiology; rMSSD is measured by the real pulsedex-dsp and nocturnal glucose by the real glucodex-dsp on the [t0,t0+dur] sleep-window slice (Clock-Contract floating ms). Apnea burden (planted AHI) is the shared latent driver; OxyDex's recovery of it is characterized separately in the ODI-4↔AHI paper. The within-patient coupling collapsing under partial | AHI is the signature of a shared driver, not a direct glucose→HRV effect. This certifies the harness's cross-node temporal coherence and that two independent detectors recover it; it does not establish a real-world CGM↔HRV effect size, which needs paired clinical CGM + RR recordings.
