How we grade it

We always tell you how sure we are.

A small mark next to every number — disc shape, never colour — tells you measurement from estimate.

measured

Read straight off the device.

validated

Checked against a standard.

emerging

Published, device-dependent.

experimental

Plausible, not confirmed.

heuristic

Rule-of-thumb direction.

🔒

Your data never leaves your hands.

Everything is read and combined on your own phone or laptop. No account, no cloud, no server holding your health. Close the tab and it's gone.
