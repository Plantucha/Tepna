How we grade it

We always tell you how sure we are.

Some numbers are rock-solid measurements. Others are smart estimates. A small mark next to every number tells you which — so you're never misled.

Measured— read straight from the sensor.
Validated— backed by published science.
Emerging— promising, still maturing.
Experimental— a useful direction, not a verdict.
Estimate— a friendly approximation.
🔒

Your data never leaves your hands.
