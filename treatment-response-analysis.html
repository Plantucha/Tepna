Synthetic ground truth — read before citing. Patients are intervention-arc subjects with a planted CPAP-start night (restricted to ≥2 pre- and ≥2 post-treatment nights so a single change-point is recoverable); flat-arc patients are the detection null. Each night is scored by the real detector — ODI-4 (oxydex-dsp) and rMSSD (pulsedex-dsp). The planted step is known and fairly clean, so this certifies that the pipeline plus a vanilla change-point detector recover and localize a treatment response; it does not establish real-world CPAP-response detection, which is noisier, confounded, and adherence-dependent.
