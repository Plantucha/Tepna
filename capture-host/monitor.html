Ensemble-average of recent beats aligned on the R-peak — noise averages out into a clean static complex. Single differential lead (H10): time intervals (ms) are valid; amplitudes are raw device µV, uncalibrated (not clinical mV) — informational, not diagnostic.

Capture streams

Only streams the device actually advertises are offered. Turning one on adds BLE traffic and disk: the estimate is measured from this host's own captures. Stream changes take effect when the device next reconnects.
