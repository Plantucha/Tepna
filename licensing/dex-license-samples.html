License & attribution stamps

Reusable, drop-in. One stylesheet, five snippets.

Load dex-license.css once, then paste the snippet for the context. Every stamp carries the same payload — author, © year, Apache-2.0, and the regulatory line — sized for where it sits. Classes are dxl- prefixed so they never collide with ans-design.css. Fonts are system monospace only; nothing is bundled or fetched.
